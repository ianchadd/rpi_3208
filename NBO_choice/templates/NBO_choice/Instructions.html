In this study, you will make decisions in each of {{Constants.num_rounds}} Decision Problems.

In each Decision Problem, your task is to earn points which will be converted to cash at the end of the experiment at a rate of X.

You earn points in each Decision Problem by choosing one from a number of strings of characters. Each string of characters is a combination of #'s and %'s. The number of points you earn in the Decision Problem is the number of #'s in the option that you choose.

An example option is displayed below:

Notice that this option has 50 characters, 28 of which are #'s. Then you would earn 28 points if you chose this option in the Decision Problem.

At the end of this study, one Decsision Problem out of the {{Constants.num_rounds}} you complete will be chosen at random and you will be paid for your points earned in that Decision Problem only. This is a bonus payment in addition to the X payment you will receive just for completing this study.
