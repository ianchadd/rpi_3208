At the beginning of each Decision Problem, you are going to be given a choice between two courses of action:
1. See {{Constants.num_options}} options, each with {{Constants.num_attributes}} characters (i.e. #'s and %'s) and earn points by choosing from those options
2. Take an outside option that earns you {{Constants.nbo_value}} points and skip seeing any displayed options

This outside option has no display and earns you {{Constants.nbo_value}} points if you choose it. Choosing the outside option effectively means that you are skipping making any choices in the Decision Problem, opting to take {{Constants.nbo_value}} points instead of earning them by selecting a displayed option.

You will be given this choice in every Decision Problem. So if you decide to take the outside option in one Decision Problem, you can still choose to see your displayed options in a later Decision Problem.
