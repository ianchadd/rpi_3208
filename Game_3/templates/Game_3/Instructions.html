Thank you for completing Game 2 - Tournament!

Overview

As in the previous two games, you will be given {{time_limit}} seconds to participate in the Counting Task. However, you will now get to choose which of the two previous payment schemes (piece rate or tournament) you prefer to apply to your performance in the third game. You will make this decision for each possible tournament prize of {{round_values}}. If you choose the piece rate, you will be paid {{piece_rate}} cents per completed box. If you choose the tournament, your performance will be evaluated relative to your group members’ past performance in Game 2 – Tournament. And, you will be paid the tournament prize only if you win the tournament (in case of ties, the winner will be randomly determined among the top performers).


Details: Determining Payments

If Game 3 - Choice is the one randomly selected for payment, then your earnings for this game are determined as follows:

  • First, the computer will randomly select the tournament prize.
  • If, for the randomly chosen prize, you chose the piece rate you receive {{piece_rate}} cents per box you complete.
  • If, for the randomly chosen prize, you chose the tournament, then your performance will be evaluated relative to your group members’ past performance in Game 2 – Tournament. If you completed more boxes than the other three group members did in Game 2 - Tournament, then you receive the chosen prize per completed box. Otherwise, you receive no earnings for this game. If there are ties, the winner will be randomly determined among the group members who tied for first place.
