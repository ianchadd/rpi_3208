Overview

As in the previous two games, you will have {{time_limit}} seconds to participate in the Counting Task. And, you will be paid the tournament prize only if you win the tournament (in case of ties, the winner will be randomly determined among the top performers).

    • The next computer screen will ask you to choose whether you want the piece rate or the tournament applied to your performance for each of the tournament prizes of {{round_values}}.
