As mentioned before, you will participate in four games. In some of these games, you will be asked to participate in the Counting Task.

In this task, you will see a series of boxes, each containing 49 ones and zeros as presented below. For each box, your job is to determine the number of zeros it contains.



In the Counting Task, your goal is to solve as many of these boxes as possible in a given amount of time. We will only tally the boxes that you solve successfully.

Next, you will see several of these boxes so that you can practice solving them.
