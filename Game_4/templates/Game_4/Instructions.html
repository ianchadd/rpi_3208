Thank you for completing Game 3 - Choice!

Overview

You do not have to solve any boxes for Game 4 - Choice-II. Instead, you will be paid based on the number of boxes you have already solved in Game 1 - Piece Rate.

You will have the option to choose which payment scheme you want. You can either choose to be paid according to the piece rate or the tournament.

  • If you choose the piece rate: you will be paid ${{piece_rate}} per solved box in Game 1.

  • If you choose the tournament: your performance in Game 1 will be evaluated relative to your group members’ performance in Game 1. And, you will be paid $1.00 only if you win the tournament (in case of ties, the winner will be randomly determined among the top performers).


    • The next computer screen will tell you how many boxes you solved in Game 1, and will ask you to choose your payment scheme.
