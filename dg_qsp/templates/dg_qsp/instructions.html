Instructions

In Part 2, you are randomly matched with another participant with the following ID: {{their_ID}}.

You will be asked to make one simple decision that can affect the payoff of {{their_ID}} for this study.

Your Decision: You will be asked how you would like to allocate {{ Constants.endowment }} between yourself and {{their_ID}}.

You can keep any amount in increments of 1 points. The remaining amount, if any, will be given to {{their_ID}}.

Please note: Due to our ID selection process, it is possible that multiple people will have the same ID. Our random matching mechanism is designed so that each participant is paid only once for this part of the study if this part is randomly selected for payment.

Also note: Our random matching mechanism might lead to multiple people being matched with {{their_ID}}. In this case, we will randomly choose one person's decision to count, so that {{their_ID}}. is only paid once for this choice. You will not know whether this is the case for your current decision, so it is in your best interest to make this decision as if it matters for {{their_ID}}'s payoff.
