This survey consists of several parts. In the first part, you will be asked to create a personal ID that is a combination of i) an 8-digit alpha-numeric string of characters and ii) a photo icon. The alpha-numeric string of characters will be randomly generated for you. You will get to choose your photo icon from a list of available images.

After setting your own ID, you will be asked a number of survey questions, some about yourself and some not.

The survey should take no longer than 10 minutes.

You will be paid $2 for completing the entire survey. This payment will occur via the Prolific platform, usually within 48 hours of completing the survey.


Please note: there will be several Attention Check questions throughout this survey meant to test whether you are paying attention. If you fail to correctly complete 1 or more of these Attention Check questions, you may not be paid.
